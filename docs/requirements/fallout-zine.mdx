# Fallout Zine

We're compiling all the Fallout projects into a magazine. The coolest ones will be featured in our print version. You'll get a physical copy if you get featured!

You get one A5 page to show off your project!

## Here's how you can make your page!

1. Download our template [HERE](https://cdn.hackclub.com/019d49b8-9834-7924-aabd-728f92bf0f3e/fallout_zine_template.pdf) (template without instruction: [variant 1](https://cdn.hackclub.com/019d49b9-11e3-7509-85e6-f1dc9b2d5150/zine_transparent.png), [variant 2](https://cdn.hackclub.com/019d49b9-f2ac-799e-980f-f29f8134e231/zine_cut_template.png))
2. Use A5 paper size in your favorite software (we recommend [Figma](https://figma.com))
3. Add everything about your project. Make sure to include the [required items](/docs/requirements/submitting-design).
4. Export as a PDF (highest/print quality) and add to your repo.
5. Add a PNG/screenshot to your README!
